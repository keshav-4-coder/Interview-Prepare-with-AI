About InterviewAI

We're on a mission to help job seekers ace their interviews and land their dream jobs through AI-powered practice and personalized feedback.

Our Story

Welcome to our Mock Interview Website, a platform designed to help users ace their job interviews with confidence. This project was created as part of our first-year university assignment by a team of four passionate students: Kishan Chaudhary, Anil Bohara, Keshav Thapa, and Aashiash Shrestha.

Our mission is to deliver a user-friendly tool for practicing mock interviews, providing realistic scenarios and constructive feedback to enhance interview skills. Kishan and Anil, our skilled backend developers, built a robust system to ensure seamless functionality and smooth performance. Meanwhile, Keshav and Aashiash, our talented frontend developers, crafted an intuitive and engaging user interface to elevate the overall experience.

Together, we blended our expertise in technology and teamwork to create a platform that not only meets our academic goals but also empowers users in their career journeys. Thank you for visiting our website—we’re excited to support you in nailing your next interview!

Who Are We?

We are Team Sutradhar, a group of passionate innovators committed to simplifying the recruitment process using Artificial Intelligence. Our goal is to bridge the gap between job seekers and recruiters with smart, efficient, and unbiased interview solutions.

Project Title: InterviewAI

What Is InterviewAI?

InterviewAI is an intelligent platform designed to automate and enhance the hiring process. It conducts AI-driven interviews, evaluates candidates using machine learning models, and provides structured feedback to recruiters.

Project Introduction

Our Mock Interview Website is a dynamic platform crafted to empower users to excel in job interviews through realistic practice. Developed as a first-year university project by Kishan Chaudhary and Anil Bohara (backend developers) and Keshav Thapa and Aashiash Shrestha (frontend developers), it offers a seamless and engaging experience. The robust backend ensures smooth functionality, while the intuitive frontend delivers interactive mock interviews and instant feedback. Our goal is to boost users’ confidence and skills for their career journeys. This project showcases our team’s passion for technology and commitment to creating a valuable tool for interview preparation.
