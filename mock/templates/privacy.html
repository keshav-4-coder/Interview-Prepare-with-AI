Last updated: May 15, 2025

Introduction

At InterviewAI, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or use our services, ensuring transparency and compliance with data protection laws.

This policy applies to all users of InterviewAIâ€™s platform, including those who sign up for accounts, subscribe to premium services, or participate in mock interviews.

Information We Collect

We gather various types of data to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, login credentials, and billing information for premium subscriptions.
  • Profile Information: Professional background, job interests, and interview preferences provided during account setup.
  • Interview Data: Questions, responses, and content generated during mock interviews.
  • Usage Data: Interactions with our website, such as features used and session duration.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and other technical details from your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Deliver and enhance our AI-powered interview preparation services.
  • Process subscriptions and payments securely.
  • Personalize interview questions and feedback.
  • Analyze usage to optimize platform performance.
  • Send updates, promotions, and service-related communications.
  • Ensure platform security and integrity.
  • Meet legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Security

We employ robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Data is stored on secure servers, and payment transactions are encrypted with SSL technology.

While we take every precaution, no internet transmission is entirely secure. We strive to protect your data, but cannot guarantee absolute security during transmission.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including legal, accounting, or reporting obligations.

Retention periods are determined based on data sensitivity, purpose, and legal requirements. You may request data deletion under certain conditions (see "Your Rights").

Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request data erasure.
  • Object to or restrict data processing.
  • Transfer your data to another service.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@interviewai.com. No fee is typically required, but we may charge for unfounded or excessive requests.

Third-Party Services

Our platform may link to third-party websites or services. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices. We recommend reviewing their policies before interacting.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Cookies we use include:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality.
  • Analytical Cookies: Track visitor counts and navigation patterns.
  • Functionality Cookies: Personalize content based on your preferences.
  • Targeting Cookies: Monitor your visits and link clicks for marketing purposes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage regular review to stay informed.
